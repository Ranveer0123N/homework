Questions and Answers (Continued)

  1. What is a wedge?

    A wedge is a simple machine with an inclined plane that tapers to a sharp edge. It is used to split objects. Examples: axe, chisel.

  2. What are the types of pulleys?
    • Fixed Pulley: A pulley with a fixed axis; it changes the direction of the applied force.
    • Movable Pulley: A pulley that reduces the effort required to lift a load.
  3. Why should machines be maintained regularly?
    • To prevent rusting and damage.
    • To ensure efficiency and reduce noise.
    • To protect moving parts from wear and tear.
  4. What are complex machines?

    Machines that combine two or more simple machines. Examples: Bicycle, Sewing Machine.
